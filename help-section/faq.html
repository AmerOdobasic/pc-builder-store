Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, select your preferred options, and then click "Add to Cart." Go to your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout."

Can I edit or cancel my order?

Orders can be edited or canceled only before they are shipped, which is at least one business day. Please contact support as soon as possible to help with this.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, international shipping is available. But, please note that shipping times vary based on the destination country.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and Interac e-Transfer.

How can I contact customer support?

Head over to our Support page and submit your question. We will get back to you within 24 hours.
