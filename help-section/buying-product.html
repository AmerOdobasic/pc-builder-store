How to Buy a Product on Amer's PC Builder Store

In this video, I will show you the entire buying process, from browsing to purchasing a product. Whether you're building your first PC or just browsing, this guide will hopefully make everything clearer.

Video Walkthrough

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Browse the All Products page.
  2. Click on a product to view its details.
  3. Select your preferred options (e.g., color or size).
  4. Click the "Add to Cart" button.
  5. Go to the Cart page to review your items.
  6. Click "Proceed to Checkout" and fill in your shipping info.
  7. If you're not logged in, you'll be asked to sign in first.
  8. Click "Place Order" to finish!
